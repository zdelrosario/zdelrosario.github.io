ASEE

The American Society for Engineering Education (ASEE), Stanford Chapter, works to promote excellence in higher education. Our chapter organizes a monthly seminar sequence, a journal club, and an annual Colloquium, all focused on the latest research and advances in education.

I serve as the Financial Officer for ASEE, handling our budget, writing funding applications, and organizing our seminar sequence.
