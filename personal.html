I'm just this guy, you know?

Hey there! My name is Zachary del Rosario. I'm a mechanical engineer, trained at Olin College of Engineering. This site serves as a portfolio of my past and current projects. Thanks for stopping by! Since you're on this page, here's a little about me...

I fly planes.

I'm a licensed private pilot. For non-aviators, that means I can legally operate an aircraft and carry passengers. I've logged upwards of 150 hours, and have flown across the country from Seattle to Boston. It was awesome.

I play bass guitar.

I've been playing for about 3 1/2 years, performing mostly classic and progressive rock pieces. I cofounded the Olin Rock Orchestra, for whom I have written numerous arrangements and with whom performed lots of crazy music.
